Connect Social Accounts

Connect your social media accounts to start scheduling and publishing posts.

API: OAuth platforms use GET /tenants/:id/connect/:platform (returns redirect URL). Instagram has two modes: instagram (via Facebook) and instagram_direct (Instagram Login API). Bluesky uses POST /connect/bluesky. Mastodon uses POST /connect/mastodon.
